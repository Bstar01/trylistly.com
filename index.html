Overview

Listly ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and operated by Benjamin Southerden. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Listly iOS application ("the App").

By using Listly, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy. We will never sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the App:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign in with Apple or Google
  • Profile information — your Etsy shop name, shop URL, and niche (only if you choose to provide them)
  • Product images — photos you take within the App are sent to our AI service to generate listings. Images are processed and immediately discarded — they are not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — features used, listings generated, and in-app actions to help us improve the App
  • Device information — device type and operating system version for debugging purposes
  • Subscription status — whether you hold an active subscription, managed by RevenueCat

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate AI-powered Etsy listings based on your product photos
  • To personalise listing results based on your shop niche
  • To manage your subscription and process renewals
  • To send important service and account notifications
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve the App (using anonymised data only)
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and user authentication
  • Google Gemini AI — AI model used to analyse product images and generate listing content. Images and prompts are processed in transit and are not stored or used for model training.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • PostHog — anonymised product analytics to understand how the App is used
  • Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In — secure, password-free authentication

Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption. Authentication credentials are stored in your device's secure Keychain and are never accessible to other apps.

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Generated listings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData and are not uploaded to our servers. If you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@trylistly.com.

Children's Privacy

Listly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
