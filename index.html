FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card?

No. The free plan is completely free with no credit card required. You only need a card if you choose to upgrade to a paid plan.

Can I connect more than one Etsy shop?

Yes. Connect unlimited Etsy stores on higher tiers, so one login runs every shop you own. The free tier connects one Etsy store so you can test every feature with no blockers.

How does Listly calculate my real profit?

We pull every fee, ad spend, shipping cost, and refund directly from Etsy. You add your COGS — by CSV or per-product — and Listly auto-calculates your true profit down to the listing level.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Listly uses Etsy's official OAuth and encrypts your data end to end. Your financials, COGS, and supplier info are only visible to you — never shared, never used for training.

I already use a research tool. Why switch?

Those are research-only tools. Listly handles research, listings, orders, and finances in one place — so you stop paying for separate apps that don't talk to each other.

What platforms are coming next?

TikTok Shop, eBay, and Shopify. One backend, every marketplace. Trial users get early access as each one ships.
