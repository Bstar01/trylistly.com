3. What Listly Does

Listly is a web-based management platform that brings everything Etsy sellers run — across one shop or many — onto a single screen. It is not a marketplace. Listly does not buy or sell goods. Current features include:

  • Connect Unlimited Stores: Link as many Etsy shops as you operate through Etsy's OAuth flow and switch between them instantly, with no per-store fees
  • Unified Dashboard: Profit, sales, orders, and average order value combined across every store or filtered to one, over custom date ranges with live trend charts
  • Order Management: Track orders across every shop, import cost of goods sold for accurate profit per order, upload tracking, export data, and sync fulfilment
  • Finance Tracking: Real profit after every fee, VAT, ad spend, and refund, reconciled against your Etsy payouts, with full breakdowns and every transaction cleanly organized
  • Listing Management: View all listings across every store in one place, bulk edit, copy listings between shops, batch activate or delete, and automate stock
  • AI Product Research: Real-time demand data plus AI-recommended titles, descriptions, and tags to help you validate a product before you list it
